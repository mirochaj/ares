Stellar models calculated with Cambridge STARS code and colours calculated by BPASS

See Eldridge & Stanway, 2009, MNRAS, 400, 1019 for details

Each file is for a different mass and metallicity of star for
filename, "newspec.zXXX.YYY" the metallicity is Z=0.XXX and the
initial mass of the star is YYY M(Sun).

Contact: jje@ast.cam.ac.uk for more details.

Columns in files are:

1) Model Timestep
2) Model age in years
3) log(R/R(Sun)
4) log(T/K)
5) log(L/L(sun))
6) M(Star)/M(Sun)
7) M(Helium Core)/M(Sun)
8) M(CO core)/M(Sun)
9) M(ONeMg core)/M(Sun)
10) M(OMg core)/M(Sun)  - **Currently not used**
11) Surface hyrdrogen abundance
12) Surface helium abundance
13) Surface Carbon abundance
14) Surface nitrogen abundance
15) Surface oxygen abundance
16) Surface neon abundance
19) M(U)
20) M(B)
21) M(V)
22) M(R)
23) M(I)
24) M(J)
25) M(H)
26) M(K)
